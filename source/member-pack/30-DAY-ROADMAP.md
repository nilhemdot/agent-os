# 🗺️ Your 30-Day Agent OS Roadmap

A simple day-by-day plan to go from "just installed" to "running an AI-first operation."

**How to use it:**
- Do **one thing a day** — 15 to 30 minutes is plenty.
- **Skip anything you don't need.** This is a buffet, not a to-do list. Nothing breaks if you skip a day.
- Each day points to a guide in the `install/` folder. Most are **free**; a few optional ones need a key (noted).
- Go at your own pace. "30 days" is a guide, not a deadline.

> 🪟 On Windows? Do the install with `install/25-WINDOWS-INSTALL.md` first, then follow the same roadmap.

---

## Week 1 — Get it running + your first wins
- **Day 1 — Install + open the dashboard.** `install/1-CORE-DASHBOARD.md` (Windows: `install/25-WINDOWS-INSTALL.md`). ✅ *Outcome: your dashboard is live at localhost:3737.*
- **Day 2 — Get the map.** Read `install/0-HOW-IT-ALL-WORKS.md` (5 min) and click around Mission Control. *Now you know what each part does.*
- **Day 3 — Connect your Obsidian vault.** `install/11-MEMORY-OBSIDIAN.md` → your notes become a living **Memory Galaxy** + give Jarvis a memory. *Your second brain is plugged in.*
- **Day 4 — Build something by voice (free).** `install/2-VOICE-BUILDING.md` — say "build me a…" and watch the Agent Factory make it on your own machine.
- **Day 5 — Meet Jarvis.** `install/3-JARVIS-VOICE.md` — a voice you talk to that answers out loud and shows you things.
- **Day 6 — Use the Pipeline.** Drop an idea in → watch it get built. *Your first real artifact.*
- **Day 7 — Review.** You have a working AI command centre. Pick the ONE tab you'll open every day.

## Week 2 — Your AI workforce
- **Day 8 — Set up Hermes.** `install/4-HERMES.md` — the agent that does multi-step jobs with real tools.
- **Day 9 — Hand Hermes a goal.** Give it a real task in Goal Mode and let it work while you do something else.
- **Day 10 — Free Claude Code.** `install/5-FREE-CLAUDE-CODE.md` — code and build for **$0** on free models.
- **Day 11 — Plug in your agent CLIs.** `install/7-AGENT-CLIS.md` — Claude, Codex, Gemini, whichever you use.
- **Day 12 — Try the AI Agent Mastermind.** Open the `/room` tab and watch agents debate a problem from different angles.
- **Day 13 — Automate one annoying task.** Pick a recurring chore and give it to Hermes.
- **Day 14 — Review.** *Goal: by now Free Claude Code + Hermes are first-class agents doing real work for you.*

## Week 3 — Your content + growth engine
- **Day 15 — Thumbnail Studio.** `install/10-THUMBNAIL-STUDIO.md` — better YouTube thumbnails with gpt-image-2. *(needs an OpenAI key)*
- **Day 16 — Radar: today's AI news → a hook.** `install/23-RADAR.md` — one click gives you a ready-to-post angle on what's breaking.
- **Day 17 — Post it.** Use a Radar story to publish your first piece of content this week.
- **Day 18 — Video Studio.** `install/12-VIDEO-STUDIO.md` — render a video or an AI avatar talking-head.
- **Day 19 — Make something fun.** Game Studio (`install/13-GAME-STUDIO.md`) or Music Studio (`install/14-MUSIC-STUDIO.md`) — great for sharing.
- **Day 20 — Find leads.** `install/22-LEADS.md` — pull real prospects + emails for outreach (free tier to start).
- **Day 21 — Review.** You now have a content + growth engine, not just a toy.

## Week 4 — Scale + automate
- **Day 22 — Paperclip: run an AI company.** `install/6-PAPERCLIP.md` — a team of AI agents with an org chart.
- **Day 23 — Give your AI company a project.** Watch the org work it end to end.
- **Day 24 — Agent Kanban.** `install/20-AGENT-KANBAN.md` — a live board your local agents work through (free, on your machine).
- **Day 25 — Loop Engineering.** `install/19-LOOP-ENGINEERING.md` — a builder acts, a judge verifies, and it repeats until the job's done.
- **Day 26 — Open Design.** `install/21-OPEN-DESIGN.md` — prototypes, decks and images, locally.
- **Day 27 — Add an extra model council.** `install/17-EXTRA-MODELS.md` — GLM, Fusion + Sakana Fugu for second opinions.
- **Day 28 — Pick a power-up.** NotebookLM (`install/15-NOTEBOOKLM.md`) or a Phone Agent (`install/9-PHONE-AGENT.md`).
- **Day 29 — Connect it all.** Run ONE workflow end to end: idea → build → content → publish.
- **Day 30 — Capstone.** Ship **one real thing** using 3+ tabs. 🎉 You're now running an AI-first operation — not just using a tool.

---

## After 30 days
- **Keep what you use, ignore the rest.** Most people live in 3–4 tabs daily and dip into the others when needed.
- **Update anytime** with `Update Agent OS.command` (Mac) or `UPDATE-WITH-AI.md` (any OS) — your settings, keys and notes are never touched.
- **Stuck on anything?** `install/8-TROUBLESHOOTING.md`, or ask any AI agent in your folder to look at it.
- **Share your Day-30 build** in the AI Profit Boardroom — seeing what others ship is half the fun. 🚀
